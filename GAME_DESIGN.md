# Game Design Overview

## Core Fantasy
Be a small rabbit exploring a giant kitchen at your own pace, guided by a gentle AI companion.

## Core Loop
Move → Jump → Collect carrots → Receive feedback → Unlock new routes

## Missions (5-Min Demo)
- Collect 5 carrots
- Reach the top counter
- Follow the ticking sound
- Complete a jump chain without falling

## Why Cozy?
- No timers
- No punishment
- Soft feedback and bounce-back mechanics

## Target Audience
Casual players, families, and players who enjoy relaxing but engaging experiences.
